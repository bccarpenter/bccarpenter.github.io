ABOUT ME/:/

  • My name is Bracken Carpenter. I grew up in a small town called Eureka, Northern California. Growing up, I was constantly surrounded by natural beauty such as ocean, mountain, and vineyards. I moved to North Carolina several years back. I never appreciated it then, but I have definitely grown to. I have been fortunate enough to travel many places and see many incredible things. I love sharing my experiences, so I decided to create this site for that sole purpose.

 

  • I got into photography around the age of 14, thanks to my grandfather. Hopefully I can inspire some of you to give it an interest. Its has been a very therapeutic form of art in my life.
