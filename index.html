WELCOME!

  • Beyond Clear is my personal website for sharing my personal experiences in nature with the world. It is my mission that my art will motivate many of you to get outside and explore the very world you reside. The pictures I share with you are my own, and I am very proud of them. When I was younger my grandfather and I would go out to a location called "Patrick's Point" in Northern California. The picture you see above is that exact spot. My grandfather helped instill in me a love for photography and an observant eye. This helped me appreciate the beauty in everything.
  • I hope this page inspires you to start observing the beauty evident, and maybe even start a photo gallery for yourselves. Hope you enjoy!
